##### Function: `floating_timestamp_day_of_week`
```
  Extract the day of the week as an integer between 0 and 6 where Sunday is 0.

Examples:

  to_floating_timestamp('2017-12-13T12:34:55').day_of_week
  -- Result: "3"




```

###### Signatures
    calendar_date -> number
