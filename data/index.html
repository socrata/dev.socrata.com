Finding Open Data

Looking for open datasets for your project, but don't know where to start? That's why we created the Open Data Network, a global search engine that allows you to search across tens of thousands of datasets from hundreds of open data catalogs. We also use machine learning to analyze datasets and consistently categorize them between catalogs.

You can go check things out at www.opendatanetwork.com or start with the search box below.

The same functionality is also available via the Discovery API.
