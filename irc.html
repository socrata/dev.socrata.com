Live Help on IRC

Generally between the hours of 9AM and 6PM Pacific time, members of the Socrata developer support team can be found in #socrata-soda on Freenode. We're also often online during major hackathons and events where we're participating. There's no guarantee that we'll be around when you come by, but it's a good way to catch us live.

Connect with the IRC client of your choice, or using the webchat below.
